# Welcome to Your AI Knowledge Base

Axio Hub is an intelligent platform that turns your static documents into an interactive AI assistant. Instead of searching through folders, you simply ask questions.

## How it Works
1. **Upload:** You provide documents (PDF, DOCX) or connect apps like Google Drive.
2. **Process:** We convert your data into "vectors" that AI can understand.
3. **Chat:** You ask questions, and our AI finds the exact answer from your data.

## Quick Start
* Go to the **Dashboard**.
* Click **"Add New Source"**.
* Upload your first PDF.
* Wait for the "Completed" notification.
* Start chatting!
